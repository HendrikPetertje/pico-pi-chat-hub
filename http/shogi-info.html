PevaPub :: Shogi Rules

What is shogi?

Shogi is Japanese chess, played on a 9x9 board. Each player starts with 20 pieces. The goal is to capture your opponent's King. The biggest difference from Western chess: when you capture a piece, it becomes yours and you can drop it back onto the board on a later turn.

The pieces

On this server, shogi pieces are shown as chess symbols. Your pieces face up, your opponent's pieces are rotated upside-down. Here's what each one does:

SymbolPieceMovement
♔ King One step in any direction
♕ Gold General One step in any direction except diagonally backward
♕ Silver General One step forward (any of 3 squares) or diagonally backward
♞ Knight Jumps two forward and one to the side (like chess, but forward only)
♖ Rook Any distance in a straight line (up, down, left, right)
♗ Bishop Any distance diagonally
♙ Lance Any distance straight forward (cannot retreat)
♟ Pawn One step straight forward

Promotion

When a piece reaches the far three rows of the board (your opponent's territory), it can be promoted. Promoted pieces turn gold on the board.

Most pieces promote to Gold General movement. The exceptions:

PiecePromotes to
RookDragon King (rook + one step diagonal)
BishopDragon Horse (bishop + one step orthogonal)
Silver, Knight, Lance, PawnMove like a Gold General

Promotion is mandatory if a piece would have no legal move without it (pawn or lance on the last row, knight on the last two rows).

Drops

When you capture an enemy piece, it goes into your hand (shown at the bottom of the screen). On any turn, instead of moving a piece on the board, you can drop a captured piece onto any empty square. Dropped pieces always return as their unpromoted form.

Drop restrictions:

• You cannot drop a pawn in a column that already has one of your unpromoted pawns.
• You cannot drop a piece where it would have no legal move (pawn/lance on last row, knight on last two rows).

Winning

Capture your opponent's King to win. There is no checkmate announcement on this server; if a King can be taken, it gets taken and the game ends.

How to play on this server

• Go to the games page and enter your name.
• Create a new game or click an existing game to join it.
• Tap one of your pieces to see where it can move (highlighted squares).
• Tap a highlighted square to move there.
• To drop a captured piece, tap it in your hand (bottom bar), then tap an empty square.
• If you don't play for 5 minutes, the game is forfeited.
